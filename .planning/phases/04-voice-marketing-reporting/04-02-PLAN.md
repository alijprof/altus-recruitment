<objective>
Slice 1 vertical slice (backend → capture UI): a recruiter on /candidates/[id] taps "Voice note", records (MicRecorder) or uploads audio, the audio is stored, a voice_notes row is created, and an Inngest function transcribes (Whisper) → extracts a structured proposal (Sonnet tool-use, D4-05 allowlist) → persists it as ready_for_review. A nightly Inngest cron soft-deletes the stored audio 30 days after capture (D4-06). After this plan a recruiter can capture a note and see it queued; the per-field approval UI lands in plan 04-03.

Output: voice_notes DB helper, Sonnet extractor, Inngest pipeline (registered), audio retention cron (registered), capture form + page, server action, and the candidate-header CTA.
